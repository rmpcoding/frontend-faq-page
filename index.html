FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

First six of your team members are free.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

You may reset your password by going into the settings and clicking, "Change Password."

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes! Feel free to cancel at any time in your profile account settings.

Do you provide any additional support?

Yes, we have our own chatbot, as well as any human assistance if you would like to reach us on our telephone line.
